Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

We keep things simple:

Some of our guides may contain affiliate or referral links to tools, courses, or services we recommend. If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we believe are genuinely useful.

Third-Party Links

Our content includes links to external websites, documentation, and tools. We're not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their policies when you visit them.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
