FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on complexity. Simple landing pages take days. Full web apps take 4-8 weeks. MVPs are typically 2-4 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline in your custom quote.

What's your payment structure?

Typically 50% upfront, 50% on completion. For larger projects, we offer milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and crypto.

Do I own the source code?

Yes, 100%. Once the project is complete and paid, you own all source code, designs, and assets. We'll transfer everything to your repositories.

Can you work with my existing codebase?

Absolutely. We can work on existing projects for maintenance, updates, new features, or refactoring. Just give us access and we'll assess what's needed.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. All projects include post-launch support (30-90 days depending on package). We also offer monthly retainer plans for ongoing maintenance and updates.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We work in iterations with regular check-ins, so issues are caught early. If something isn't right, we'll revise until it is. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign NDAs before discussing your project details. Confidentiality is important to us, and we treat all client information with discretion.

Where are you located?

We work remotely and serve clients worldwide. We're flexible with time zones and schedule calls at times that work for you.
